About

Bikramjit Singh has spent most of his career inside QuarkXPress — first the typography engine, then the cross-platform migrations, more recently the layer that lets modern web frameworks render inside decades-old C++ desktop code. The systems he works on are the kind that take years to mature, which is part of why he has stayed.

His work spans the integration layer between native and web stacks: C++ and Objective-C desktop applications modernized through C# adapters and Angular components embedded via CEF. The systems he owns are foundational and high-stakes — typography subsystems, format recovery, Unicode infrastructure for nearly ten Indian scripts, and the migration of legacy SOAP integrations to REST microservices. Twenty-three years in, recently certified as an Azure AI Engineer.
